FAQ (V1 / Beta)

You can log in using:
  • Discord OAuth
  • Custom Profile Login (Name + PFP + Auto-Login via IP)
Some login features may be unstable due to Beta.
2. Where do I get support?
Open a support ticket at: khxzi.com/support
(Support system is under development ‚Äî response may be limited.)
3. How do I verify my account?
Go to: khxzi.com/verify
Verification logs are sent to Discord automatically.
4. How do I chat with the community?
Use the "Chat With Community" button on the dashboard. It connects you to Discord general chat via Webhooks.

Chat system is currently under development and may not fully work.
Join Discord directly here: khxzi.com/discord
5. How do I get Premium?
Go to: /premium
Enter a Premium Key or get one at our Discord.

Premium includes:
  • No ads
  • Custom domain: username.khxzi.com
  • Premium badge
  • Special integrations
  • Priority support
6. What does V1/Beta mean?
Since you're using Beta, some features may:
  • Break randomly
  • Not load
  • Have "Under Development" tags
  • Be unavailable depending on your account type

Staff use their @khxzi.com emails & passwords to access:

Owners can also:
  • Generate staff accounts

  • Generate Premium Keys
  • Manage system data

  • Earn coins
  • Earn XP
  • Unlock badges
But Shop, More games, and Rewards are under development.
